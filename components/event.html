    Welcome To Our Event

    RTC is an inaugural event organized by the IEEE RAS ENSIT Student Branch and the IEEE RAS Tunisia Chapter. This conference is designed to bring together students, professionals, and enthusiasts to explore cutting-edge advancements in robotics and automation.

    The theme for this edition is Industry 6.0, aiming to engineer imagination into reality by diving into topics like augmented and virtual reality, digital twin technology, AI in industrial processes, and IoT's role in creating interconnected environments.

    Meet Our Speakers

    Meet the experts and pioneers in robotics, AI, and Industry 6.0 who will share their insights and experiences. Our distinguished speakers come from diverse backgrounds, offering a wealth of knowledge in cutting-edge technology and innovation.

    Discover Our Workshops

    Engage in hands-on workshops designed to deepen your understanding of AI, VR, and robotics. These sessions provide practical experience in applying advanced technologies to real-world industrial challenges.

    Awards Ceremony

    Recognizing excellence in robotics, the IEEE RAS Tunisia Conference will honor outstanding student branches and individuals. Awards will be presented for innovative projects and significant contributions to the field, celebrating the best in robotics and technology.

    Meet Our Sponsors

    Our sponsors are leaders in technology and innovation, supporting the advancement of robotics and AI. Explore their contributions and learn how they are shaping the future of Industry 6.0.
